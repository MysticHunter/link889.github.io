Facts

The last time the volcano erupted was 2014.
It is 16,480 feet tall. Tungurahue is a stratovolcano. Stratovolcanoes build up a lot of pressure before realizing the magma and ash making huge gas clouds.

You will need these five things to stay safe in a volcano eruption.
  1. food
  2. water
  3. first aid kit
  4. radio
  5. protection for eyes and lungs (goggles or face mask)
